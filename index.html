Our services

Mentoring

Our mentoring has helped many individuals and families write a unique life-changing journey to Canada. Limited spots.

Transfer / Day use

Our Ontario team is ready to welcome you upon your arrival. We provide transfer service to several cities in the province of Ontario. We offer Day Use service to our clients in Toronto and London. The Day Use service is fully customized according to your preferences. We can help you with your first purchases, open a bank account, issue a SIN Number, purchase a public transport pass, hire a cell phone line, enjoy the city and talk a lot about tips for the first days and curiosities of the city.
